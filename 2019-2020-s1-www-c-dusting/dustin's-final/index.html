Phalanx





See Project
Phalanx, a laptop case with mechanized tentacles that rap and protect the laptop from falls. This revolutionary new idea utilizes epoxy resin soft robot tentacles to save you expensive laptop from falls at any height. Made in the Nuvu studio at MIT it uses arduino to prectic falls and to deploy the tentacle.

The Flavor Fork





See Project
In my studio we were tasked at making food and dining in space better for the people on the I.S.S., so I created the Flavor Fork. The Flavor Fork is a new innovation in zero gravity dining. It allows astronauts to enhance the flavor of the bland and repetitive foods in space. By letting you put sauces and flavor right on your taste buds as you eat, it allows a more flavorful and intuitive experience to flavor enhancers in space.
