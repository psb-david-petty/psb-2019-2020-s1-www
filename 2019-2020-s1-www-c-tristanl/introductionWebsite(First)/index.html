Cookie Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Combine in a small bowl.
    • 2 3/4 cups flour
    • 1 tsp baking soda
    • 1 tsp salt
  3. Beat in a large mixing bowl until creamy.
    • 1 Cup (2 sticks) of butter
    • 3/4 cups granulated sugar
    • 3/4 cups packed brown sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Add 2 large eggs to the large mixer bowl one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  5. Gradually beat in the flour mixture.
  6. Stir in 2 cups NESTLÉ® TOLL HOUSE® Semi-Sweet Chocolate Morsels
  7. Form small balls of the dough, and place onto an ungreased cookie sheet.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown, and let cool after taking out of the oven.
