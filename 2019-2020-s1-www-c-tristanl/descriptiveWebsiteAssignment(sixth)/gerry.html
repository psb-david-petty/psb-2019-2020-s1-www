Gerry is a font created from congressional districts all across the United States. In most places, districts have been Gerrymandered so far that the districs can be formed into clear letters. This font was created as a way to bring attention to the issue of Gerrymandering, and can be downloaded on the official website located here: https://www.uglygerry.com/.
