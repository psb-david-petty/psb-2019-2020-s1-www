Guinea pigs are vegetarian. The best foods for them are various forms of grasses and vegetables.

Timothy hay

Timothy hay is the healthiest food for guinea pigs. “It is excellent for your pet's teeth and digestive tract. Timothy hay should be provided to your guinea pig at all times …to help with digestion and oral health. A constant supply of hay is also great for your pet's mental well-being, since grazing and chewing is soothing.” — Sweet Meadow Farm

Pellets

Guinea-pig pellets are vegetable-based and contain additional nutrients, but should only supplement hay for your pet. Oxbow's Essentials “…complete and balanced adult guinea pig food contains Timothy hay for healthy digestion and all the wholesome ingredients and vital nutrients needed to support daily function and performance.” — Oxbow Animal Health

Vegetables

Raw vegetables are also good for your guinea pig. Guinea pig favorites include parsley and carrots. Like pellets, green vegetables should only supplement hay for your pet.
