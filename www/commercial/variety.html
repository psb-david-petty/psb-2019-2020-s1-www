Short-haired cavies

Breeds of short-haired guinea pigs include: American guinea pig, Crested guinea pig, Teddy guinea pig, Rex guinea pig, Agouti guinea pig.

Long-haired cavies

Breeds of long-haired guinea pigs include: Peruvian guinea pig, Abyssinian guinea pig, Silkie guinea pig, Texel guinea pig, Merino guinea pig.

Capybaras

Capybaras are not guinea pigs, but are in the Caviidae family and so are also cavies. "The capybara (Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris) …is the largest living rodent in the world. …Adult capybaras grow to 106 to 134 cm (3.48 to 4.40 ft) in length, stand 50 to 62 cm (20 to 24 in) tall at the withers, and typically weigh 35 to 66 kg (77 to 146 lb)…" — Wikipedia.
