Recipe for Buffalo Chicken Dip

Image From theforkedspoon

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hot Sauce
  • Garlic Powder
  • Green Onion
  • Cheese (Chedder and Mozzerella)

Directions

  1. Heat the cream cheese, ranch dressing, hot sauce, pepper, and garlic powder in a small pot over medium-low heat (tip- if your cream cheese is fresh out of the refrigerator, microwave for 10-15 seconds to soften up a bit before adding to the pot). Whisk constantly until combined and creamy. Remove from heat.
  2. Stir in the green onion, shredded chicken, one cup of the shredded cheddar cheese and one cup of the shredded mozzarella cheese.
  3. Transfer mixture to a baking pan and sprinkle with remaining shredded cheese. Bake until golden and bubbly.
