Tree Species

Within the vast and diverse realm of all the different types of trees, there are two groups that they can be catagorized in: deciduous and evergreen. They're both really cool, and have interesting properties that make them unique from one another. In addition to both of those groups, there are semi-deciduous trees too, which share characteristics of the evergreens and deciduous.


Deciduous

Characteristics

A deciduous tree is a type of tree that sheds its leaves annually. Deciduous trees are the trees that leaves turn different colours as winter approaches.
