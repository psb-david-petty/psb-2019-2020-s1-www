Trees!

Hopefully you know and understand how important trees are to this planet that we live on. They create habitats for other animals, clean up CO2, a harmful greenhouse gas, from the atmosphere, and they generate oxygen in the process of creating their energy. One tree produces 260 pounds of oxygen every year.

Trees are in danger.

Forest fires, deforestation, harmful toxins, such as pesticides, all are damaging to trees and their ecosystem.

Y'all better protect trees.
