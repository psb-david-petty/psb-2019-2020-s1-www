Recipe

How to make brigadeiro (a delicious Brazilian treat).

Image from Laylita

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 14 ounces sweetened condensed milk

Directions

  1. Mix cocoa, butter, and condensed milk in medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. Cook for 10 minutes until thickened, while stirring
  3. Let it cool
  4. Roll into small balls
  5. Serve immediately, or chill until served
