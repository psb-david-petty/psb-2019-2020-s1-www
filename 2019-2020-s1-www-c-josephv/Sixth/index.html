The Boring Company "Not A Flamethrower" is a flamethrower. It is essentially an airsoft gun that has been fitted with a blowtorch. 20,000 Boring Company flamethrowers were released in 2018, all of which sold out within a few days.

The Boring Company "Not a Flamethrower" is a flamethrower. It is essentially an airsoft gun that has been fitted with a blowtorch. This design does defend the claim of the product as Merriam Webster defines a flamethrower as "a device that expels from a nozzle a burning stream of liquid or semiliquid fuel under pressure." The Not A Flamethrower, however, uses propane to burn at a much shorter range, truly not throwing any flame.
