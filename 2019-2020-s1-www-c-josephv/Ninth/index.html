Cosmo Luigi Viola

The most beautius pug in the land

About

Cosmo Luigi Viola is a beautiful, squishy, little pug who currently resides in Brookline, MA. His favorite activities include sleeping, eating, chewing bones, going to the beach in RI, long walks by the water, and playing chasing games. He is seven years old and in good health. He does have addison's disease which requires him to take a daily pill and monthly injection. He is still very active and happy, though.

ORIGINS

Cosmo was born on May 21st, 2012. He is is the son of Jack and Cheerio, both pure bred show dogs, Jack of which has won many awards at numerous dog shows. My family officially decided on adopting a dog in March of 2012, after my sister and I broke down crying, upset that all of our close friends had dogs, except for us. In April, we decided on a pug, a small, cute, and relatively easy dog to take care of. My mom got in contact with a breeder who was expecting a litter very soon, the puppies of which would be ready in August

Cosmo nowadays

Cosmo is now 7 years old, and is in good health. He is still very friendly and loves to meeet everybdoy. He is less energetic than he used to be, but will still get into running around if prompted to do so. He is better behaved than he used to be, with much fewer accidents, but he will still get into troube from time to time. He is very close to all of us and gets lots of love from the whole family.
